Brand Value


12 years experience in skateboard manufacturing has given Penny to be a leading board company in Australia. Penny is well known for its great quality in manufacturing and design skateboard. As a Penny board is an icon of young spirit and fashion, Penny's brand value will be being sporty in a fashionalbe way. The choice of the strapline, "Explore your potential" is well complying with this value as it encourages people to be ambitious - especially young aged groups. Penny Australia will make sure follow the brand guidelines to keep its consistency and to be exploring people's potential.
